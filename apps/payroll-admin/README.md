# Payroll Admin (Fee Management)

Focused interface for fee management and collection.

## Features

- ✅ Fee structure management
- ✅ Student fee assignment
- ✅ Payment recording
- ✅ Receipt generation
- ✅ Fee collection reports
- ✅ Pending fee tracking
- ✅ Discount management
- ✅ Bulk payment processing

## Access

**Role Required:** `payroll_admin` or `admin`

## Port

Development: `http://localhost:3003`

## Design

- Focused on fee operations only
- No access to transport, inventory, or student management
- Streamlined workflow for fee collection
